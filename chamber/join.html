Membership Levels

NP Membership

For non-profit organizations. Includes directory listing and event access.

Bronze Membership

Includes directory listing, event access, and basic advertising opportunities.

Silver Membership

Includes all Bronze benefits plus enhanced advertising and networking perks.

Gold Membership

Includes all Silver benefits plus premium placement and sponsorship options.

NP Membership Benefits

Designed for non-profit organizations with no membership fee. Includes directory listing, access to community events, and basic networking opportunities.

Perfect for organizations focused on community impact and visibility.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Includes NP benefits plus additional exposure through our online directory and select event promotions.

Ideal for small businesses starting to grow their local presence.

Silver Membership Benefits

Includes all Bronze benefits plus enhanced advertising, featured directory placement, and invitations to member-only trainings.

Great for businesses looking to expand their network and visibility.

Gold Membership Benefits

Includes all Silver benefits plus premium homepage spotlight positions, sponsorship opportunities, and priority event access.

Best for organizations seeking maximum exposure and leadership in the community.
