About Me

I'm originally from Mexico and I’ve always loved learning new things. Recently I’ve been teaching myself piano and guitar, cooking for my family, playing with my kids, reading, and traveling. I once studied to become a commercial pilot and was close to finishing my aviation degree, but financial challenges made me pause that dream.

I’ve traveled to many countries, experienced different cultures and cuisines, and met incredible people along the way. I love web design and drawing, and after earning my computer science degree, I’m now working toward my bachelor’s.
